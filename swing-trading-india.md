# Swing Trading — Indian Market

## What Is It

Hold stocks 2–10 days. Catch one "swing" — bottom to top of a move. Not intraday, not long-term.

---

## Best Time to Trade

### Time of Day (NSE/BSE — 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM IST)

| Window | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **9:15–9:45 AM** | High volatility, big gaps. Risky for entry, good for reading direction |
| **10:00–11:30 AM** | Best entry window. Trend established, volume confirms |
| **2:00–3:00 PM** | Second good window. Institutions rebalance |
| **3:15–3:30 PM** | Avoid. Erratic, stop-hunt zone |

### Days of Week

- **Tuesday–Thursday** — cleanest setups
- **Monday** — gap risk from weekend news
- **Friday** — positions squared off, low conviction

### Avoid

- Budget day, RBI policy day, F&O expiry (last Thu of month) — unpredictable spikes
- Earnings week of the stock (unless trading the event)

## Best Sectors for Swing Trading (India)

### High Volatility = Good Swings

| Sector | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **IT (TCS, Infy, HCL)** | Tracks US markets, clean technical moves |
| **Banking & Finance (HDFC Bank, ICICI, Axis)** | High liquidity, responds to RBI news |
| **Metals (Tata Steel, JSW, SAIL)** | Commodity cycles = big swings |
| **Pharma (Sun, Dr Reddy, Cipla)** | USFDA news = sharp moves |
| **Auto (M&M, Maruti, Tata Motors)** | Sales data monthly = catalyst |
| **PSU Stocks (NTPC, BEL, HAL)** | Govt capex theme = trending moves |

### Avoid for Swing

- FMCG (too stable, small swings)
- Penny stocks (low liquidity, manipulated)

## Setup / Strategy

### Entry Signals

- **Breakout** — stock breaks resistance on high volume
- **Pullback to support** — stock in uptrend, dips to 20 EMA, bounce
- **RSI divergence** — price makes new low, RSI doesn't (reversal signal)

### Key Indicators

```
EMA 9, 20 — trend direction
RSI (14) — overbought >70, oversold <30
MACD — crossover confirmation
Volume — must confirm the move
```

### Risk Management

- **Stop loss** — 2–3% below entry (hard rule)
- **Target** — 5–8% (minimum 2:1 reward:risk)
- **Position size** — max 10–15% capital per trade
- **Max open trades** — 3–5 at once

## Key Indian Market Factors to Track

- **FII activity** — if FIIs buying, uptrend strong
- **Delivery %** — high delivery = genuine buying, not speculation
- **RBI meetings** — 6 per year, move banking stocks
- **US Fed meetings** — IT + market-wide impact
- **Monthly F&O expiry** — last Thu, avoid holding through it

---

## Simple Swing Trading Routine

1. **Sunday evening** — scan for setups using Chartink
2. **Monday morning** — check FII data, global cues
3. **10 AM Tuesday** — enter if setup confirms
4. **Daily** — trail stop loss as stock moves up
5. **Hit target or stop** — exit, log trade

---

## Golden Rule

Trade with trend of Nifty. Nifty down = don't go long on individual stocks. Wait for alignment.
