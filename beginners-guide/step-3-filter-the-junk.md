# Step 3 — Filter the Junk

The scanner gives you a raw list. Not every stock on it is good. This step removes the risky ones quickly, so you don't waste time looking at bad charts.

This takes about 2 minutes. Just scan the list and remove anything that fails any one of these checks.

---

## Check 1: Price above ₹100

Look at the "Close" price column in the scan results.

- Below ₹100 → remove it

Why: Very cheap stocks (called penny stocks) are often controlled by operators who pump the price and then dump, leaving normal people with losses. They look attractive but are traps.

---

## Check 2: High daily volume

Look at the "Volume" column.

- Volume below 1 lakh (100,000) shares per day → remove it

Why: If very few shares trade each day, you might not be able to sell when you want to. You could get stuck holding a stock with no buyers.

A simple way to check: if the volume number has fewer than 6 digits, it's too low.

---

## Check 3: Market cap above ₹1,000 crore

Look at the "Market Cap" column if available. Or just Google the company name + "market cap."

- Market cap below ₹1,000 crore → remove it (be careful with these)
- Market cap below ₹500 crore → definitely remove it

Why: Very small companies have less information available, are more easily manipulated, and can fall very sharply. Bigger companies are more stable.

---

## Check 4: Is this a real company you can find information about?

Google the company name quickly.

- Can't find any news, website, or information about it → remove it
- Company is in a business that makes no sense or you can't understand → skip it for now

You don't need to deeply understand every business. But you should be able to find it easily.

---

## What you'll be left with

After these 4 checks, your list of 20–40 stocks usually becomes 5–12 stocks. That's a perfectly manageable number to check charts for in Step 4.

---

## Quick filter table

| Check | Keep | Remove |
|---|---|---|
| Price | Above ₹100 | Below ₹100 |
| Volume | Above 1 lakh shares/day | Below 1 lakh |
| Market cap | Above ₹1,000 crore | Below ₹500 crore |
| Findability | Can Google it | No information exists |

If in doubt, remove it. There are always more stocks to look at tomorrow.
