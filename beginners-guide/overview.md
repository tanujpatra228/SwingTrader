# How to Find Stocks for Swing Trading — Overview

Swing trading = buy a stock, hold a few days to a few weeks, sell when it goes up.

The steps below tell you exactly how to go from 2,000+ stocks → 1 good trade.

---

## The 6 Steps

| Step | What you do | Time needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| [Step 1 — Check the Market](step-1-check-the-market.md) | Is NIFTY healthy? If no, stop here. | 2 min |
| [Step 2 — Run a Scanner](step-2-run-a-scanner.md) | Use free Chartink scans to get a raw list | 5 min |
| [Step 3 — Filter the Junk](step-3-filter-the-junk.md) | Remove bad stocks from the list | 2 min |
| [Step 4 — Read the Charts](step-4-read-the-charts.md) | Find stocks in a "ready to move" position | 10 min |
| [Step 5 — Wait for the Trigger](step-5-wait-for-trigger.md) | Only buy when the stock actually breaks out | ongoing |
| [Step 6 — Plan Your Exit](step-6-plan-your-exit.md) | Set stop loss and target BEFORE buying | 3 min per trade |

## The golden rule

Do these steps **every evening** in order. Takes 20 minutes. Never skip Step 1.

The whole point is: you are NOT trying to predict which stock will go up. You are just finding stocks that are already going up, resting briefly, and waiting for them to continue. Let the market do the work.
