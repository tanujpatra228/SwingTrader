# Step 1 — Check the Market

Before looking at any individual stock, check if the overall market is healthy.

Why? Because 70% of stocks follow the market direction. Even a great stock falls when the market is falling. This one step alone saves most beginners from most of their losses.

---

## How to do it

Open **TradingView** (free at tradingview.com). Search for **NIFTY 50**. Make sure you are on the **daily chart** (click "1D" at the top).

Add this indicator: search "AP AI1" by author "ank5956" → add it. It draws coloured lines on your chart automatically.

Now look at three things:

---

## Thing 1: Is price above the blue line? (20 EMA)

The blue line follows the average price of the last 20 days. Think of it as the "short-term health line."

- Price above the blue line → stock/market is healthy short-term ✓
- Price below the blue line → not healthy ✗

---

## Thing 2: Is the blue line above the green line? (50 EMA)

The green line is the average of the last 50 days. Think of it as the "medium-term trend line."

- Blue line above green line → the trend is going up ✓
- Blue line below green line → the trend is going down ✗

---

## Thing 3: Are both lines pointing upward?

If both lines are sloping upward (not flat, not curving down), the market is in a clean uptrend.

---

## What to conclude

| What you see | What it means | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| Price above blue, blue above green, both pointing up | Strong uptrend | Look for stocks to buy. Full confidence. |
| Price just crossed above the blue line from below | Early recovery | Look carefully, take small trades only |
| Price bouncing between the blue and green lines | Choppy, uncertain | Build watchlist. Very small trades or none. |
| Price below both blue and green lines, both pointing down | Downtrend | Do not buy anything. Sit in cash. |

---

## Example: reading today's NIFTY (Jul 15, 2026)

NIFTY is at 24,068. The 20 EMA is at ~24,035. The 50 EMA is at ~23,954.

- Price is above the blue line, barely ✓
- Blue line is above green line ✓
- But NIFTY just fell sharply from 24,547 a few days ago — that's a warning sign

**Verdict: Caution zone.** The numbers look okay but the recent fall shows weakness. Do not take new trades until NIFTY closes above ~24,300 convincingly. Build your watchlist in the meantime.

---

## Remember

You are not trying to predict what NIFTY will do. You are just checking: is the environment safe for buying right now? If yes, move to Step 2. If no, stop here and come back tomorrow.
