# Step 2 — Run a Scanner

A scanner is a tool that automatically checks all 2,000+ NSE stocks against a set of rules and gives you only the ones that pass. Instead of checking every stock manually, the scanner does the hard work in seconds.

---

## Where to go

Open **chartink.com** in your browser. It is free. You do not need to log in to view results.

---

## Which scans to run

Ankur Patel has shared these ready-made scans. Click the link, wait a few seconds, you get a list of stocks.

### Scan A — Stocks resting quietly after a rise
**Link:** chartink.com/screener/3-week-tight-close-19

What it finds: stocks that went up strongly in the past few weeks, and are now moving in a very tight, narrow range for 3 weeks. These are the best setups — like a spring being compressed before it releases.

Run this: **once a week, on Sunday evening.**

---

### Scan B — Stocks pulling back to their trend line
**Link:** chartink.com/screener/ankur-patel-s-20-ema-scan

What it finds: stocks that are in an uptrend, but have dipped close to their 20-day average line. This is a "buy the dip" setup — the stock is healthy but taking a small rest.

Run this: **every evening.**

---

### Scan C — Stocks quietly contracting (Ankur's daily RC scans)
**Links:**
- chartink.com/screener/ema-scan-2-7
- chartink.com/screener/new-daily-2045

What they find: stocks that have had a strong rise recently AND today moved only a tiny amount (less than 4.5% up or down). The stock is above its 20-day average line and just sitting still. This is the early sign of a base forming.

These are Ankur's **most important daily scans** — he runs both every evening.

Run this: **every evening.**

---

### Scan D — Stocks where big money stepped in recently
**Link:** chartink.com/screener/ankur-s-volume-scan

What it finds: stocks that had a big price move AND traded with 3× more volume than their usual average. This means institutions were buying.

**Important:** do NOT buy these directly. These stocks just made a big move. They need to rest and form a base first (Step 4). Use this scan to find which stocks are attracting big money, then **watch and wait** for them to consolidate before entering.

Run this: **every evening, as a watchlist-building reference.**

---

### Scan E — Stocks already moving strongly
**Link:** chartink.com/screener/momentum-stocks-797

What it finds: stocks that have risen 20%+ in the last 5 days or 30%+ in the last 30 days. These are already in momentum. You won't buy them directly from this scan — you use this list to find which **sectors** are leading right now. If Defence stocks dominate this list, Defence is the hot sector this week.

Run this: **every evening, as a sector-reference only.**

---

## What you get after running scans

Each scan gives you a table of stocks — maybe 10 to 40 names. Do not be overwhelmed. You are not buying all of them. This is just your raw list.

Write down or copy the names. Now go to Step 3 to cut this list down.

---

## Tip: start with just one scan

If this feels like too much, start with just **Scan A (3-week tight close)** run once a week. That single scan is often enough to find good setups as a beginner.
